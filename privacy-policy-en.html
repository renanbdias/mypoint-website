MyPoint respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information.

    Data Collection:

  • MyPoint collects data related to your workouts and game points to sync with the Health app and provide relevant information to you.

    Data Usage:

  • The data collected is used solely to display your saved workouts and game points. This data is never shared with third parties and remains private to the user.

    Third-Party Sharing:

  • MyPoint does not share your data with any third parties. All data is used exclusively within the app for user benefit.

    User Rights:

  • Users have complete control over their data. You can access, delete, or correct your data at any time through the app settings.

    Data Security:

  • MyPoint employs iCloud security measures to protect your data. All data is stored locally on your device and in iCloud.

    Children's Privacy:

  • Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children or users under a certain age. The use of our app is intended for individuals who are of legal age to use iCloud services.

    Changes to Privacy Policy:

  • Any changes to this Privacy Policy will be communicated through the app. Users are encouraged to review the policy periodically.

    Contact Information:

  • If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at renanbenattidias@icloud.com.
